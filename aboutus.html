Our Sports Club Team Members

President

Action :

Setting goals, representing the club to the university, and ensuring the club’s activities align with its mission.

Vice President

Action :

Assists the president in their duties and often steps in if the president is unavailable.

Secretary

Action :

Responsible for administrative tasks such as maintaining meeting minutes, handling official correspondence, and keeping records of club activities.

Treasurer

Action :

Manages the club’s finances, including budgeting, tracking expenses, also works to secure sponsorships.

Team Captain

Action :

Managing team practices, motivating teammates, and acting as a liaison between the team and the larger club leadership.

Events Coordinator

Action :

Organizes and oversees the scheduling of events, competitions, and social gatherings.

Public Relations(PR) Officer

Action :

Handles the club's social media accounts, marketing, ensuring that members have a positive experience within the club, and resolving conflicts.

Volunteer

Action :

Organizing events, managing logistics, helping with training sessions, and fostering a welcoming atmosphere for both new and existing members.
